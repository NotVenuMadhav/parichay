Our Story

Having our roots and relations established all the way from our schooling days, and fast-forwarding to the timeline of covid-19 pandemic lockdown, Parichay was just an idea which acutely sparked in a friendly discussion. This idea propelled into our quaratine and lockdown-driven minds and lifestyle, which developed into this venture of podcasts.

Our idea is simple yet effective in its broadest sense. Every individual has some Story which they might have experienced in their lifetime or are working towards a motive to help a certain someone. Distributing opinions, sharing perspectives, generalizing view points among viewers and listeners creates an improved idea and a vision to look at issues, hurdles or obstacles in a much polished and cultivated manner.

We strive to deliver these ideas as your story tellers through our podcasting sub-chain, MeraParichay. We want to be India's leading, unbiased, purely rich and perspective-driven podcasters with a complete view to provide to our general public with hardly experienced ideas or unknown ventures.
